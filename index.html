I recently graduated with a Masters in Computer Engineering at North Carolina State University. I worked under the guidance of Dr. Aydin Aysu at Hector Lab and Dr. Chau-Wai Wong.

My research primarily focusses on enhancing the security and privacy of machine learning, with special emphasis on developing light-weight private-yet-efficient solution for edge devices.

I developed an approximate variational solution for user privacy against gradient inversion-based attacks.
